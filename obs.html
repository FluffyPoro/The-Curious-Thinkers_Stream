  • What Is OBS?

    Open Broadcaster Software, or OBS for short, is a free open-source platform used for offline video recording and live streaming.

  • Why did we use OBS?

    It's easy, beginner friendly, free, and has a bunch of features we can use for streaming.

  • OBS features

    Screen capture, in-game audio capture, two-way sound capture, 4K video, scene composition, audio mixing and supports streaming on various platforms like YouTube, Twitch and Facebook.
