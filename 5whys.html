5 Why's

Why was the content type ‘Interview’ chosen?

1st

Interviews can help gather opinions about the topic to help improve it if needed. It gives insights to both the interviewer and interviewee.

2nd

It helps to give further insights on the student’s preferences.

3rd

It helps us accurately differentiate how students learn better in some ways.

4th

Interviewers can give suggestions on the topic and help explain, better understand and explore the research topic.

5th

It helps us deep dive into the lives of the interviewees and get a better and open look their experiences.
