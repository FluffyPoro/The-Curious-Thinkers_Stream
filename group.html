About Us

We aim to conduct a comparative analysis between online and onsite learning and the difference of its impacts on the overall productivity of Grade 10 APEC students, along with examining the key factors such as academic performance, adaptability to different learning environments, time management, and sleep. The research will determine which mode of learning more effectively enhances the students productivity and identify the advantages and disadvantages associated with both learning modalities.
